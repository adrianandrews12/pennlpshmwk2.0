My name is Adrian Andrews, I have recently started my journey to becoming a successful web developer. I am currently a Temple University undergrad student, I was supposed to be starting my junior year, but I decided to defer for the year. This allowed me some time to self-reflect on what I really wanted to do with my life. Previously I was studying kinesiology but I came to the realization that that wasn't something that I genuninely wanted to do with my life.


During my first year off from school I used that time as an opportunity to try and find my niche. This pretty much consisted of me working various jobs to see where I felt most comfortable in. Although all of these jobs have undeniably progressed me down the truly never ending path towards my self-actualization, I felt no genuine passion for any of them. They all felt temporary and completely dull. This brings me to how I got into coding. Through a somewhat close friend I was able to come across a web development program being hosted by University of Pennsylvania.


Coding to me at that point was something that although admititngly I had little to no experience in, the experience I did have in it brought me more joy in doing it than anything else I ever attempted. It always felt like art to me, to the untrained eye it may seem unnecessarily convoluted and pragmatic when in reality its a multi-dimensional world contantly metamorphosing itself. I hope by the end of the programs I have enough knowledge to properly navigate through such a dynamically rich field.
